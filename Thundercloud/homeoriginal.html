Flooded with Discovery Documents? We can help!

Your law firm can receive thousands of documents with each production response. Information pours in, all in different formats: PDF, docx, xlsx, PST, ZIP, emails, and many more. Traditional procedures to organize and query individual documents are quickly overwhelmed.

You need a new way to organize these documents. And you need a better way to query files and to be able to retrieve a document by word or phrase quickly.

Welcome to ThunderCloud Resources - we have a solution for you!

One Searchable Database Your New Reality

Imagine all your case documents organized and searchable in one electronic database that you can query however you want, wherever you are. We will replace your existing fragmented search process with our streamlined integrated system.

Duplicate pages and duplicate files will be identified and all files, even non-searchable picture files, are retained in your database. We specialize in better quality, more accurate conversion of documents into a custom, electronically searchable database.

Why ThunderCloud

A Better Process: Better Results

Take advantage of our expertise to affordably outsource your discovery document analysis needs. You will receive high quality electronically searchable documents, without having to buy costly equipment or software or use valuable storage space or time to achieve.
