Join the AATSP Today Benefits for You and Your Students

The American Association of Teachers of Spanish and Portguese is a National Organization founded in 1917. Membership is open to all teachers of Spanish and Portuguese as well as all others interested in the languages and the cultures. Over 70 local chapters of the AATSP exist in the United States and Canada. Your membership in the AATSP will benefit your classroom and your students.

Benefits

AATSP provides resources to teachers through a variety of programs. An Annual Conference provides an opportunity to attend a wide range of sessions, workshops, academic panels, and professional development events. The AATSP recognizes and gives awards to individuals who have done outstanding work in the teaching and promotion of Spanish language education. It also provides scholarships for first-time conference attendees and study/research abroad opportunities.

Classroom resources are available in online thematic units based on authentic materials in Spanish and Portuguese. The units consist of lesson plans, student worksheets, activities, and suggested assessments. Enlace is a quarterly online newsletter containing advice for all Spanish teaching professionals. Your AATSP membership also includes automatic enrollment into your local chapter.

Hispania

The official journal of the AATSP devoted to the teaching of Spanish and Portuguese. This journal features articles on literature and language, book reviews, theoretical and applied linguistics, professional and Hispanic/Luso-Brazilian World news, and media and computers, along with AATSP official announcements, and advertisements. The journal also offers solutions and tips for the classroom.

Hispania is published in March, June, September and December.
