Awards & Scholarships

The AATSP offers annual awards to its members in a variety of categories as well as a number of travel scholarships and stipends. In addition, there are some awards for your students as well. All of the information about this year's awards can be found on the national AATSP website.

AATSP.org

Current Award Nominations Open

Sociedad Honoraria Hispánica Awards 2017 - The applications for all Sociedad Honoraria Hispánica awards are now available. Deadline for all categories is April 1, 2017

  • Chapter of the Year - The SHH presents up to five Chapter of the Year Awards annually to chapters based on the quality and quantity of activities, number of contributions to Albricias and exemplary activities in promoting the goals of the SHH. Chapter sponsors who receive the Chapter of the Year Award are presented with $150 and a plaque.

  • Activity of the Year - The SHH holds an annual contest to honor the best chapter activities of the year. Awards are given in two categories: short-term and long-term activities. Winners receive $100, $75, or $50 depending on their placement in the contest. Up to six total awards may be given.

  • Mario Fierros Award for Chapter Sponsor of the Year - The Mario Fierros Award for Excellence is awarded to an SHH chapter sponsor who has given exemplary service to the SHH during the year. Chapter sponsors who have been a member of the SHH Advisory Board during the last eight years are ineligible. The awardee receives a plaque and a trip to the AATSP annual conference. Reimbursed expenses include travel and lodging during the conference as well as meals. A sponsor can only receive this award once.

  • Park-Sihocky Award for New Chapter Sponsor - The Park-Sihocky Award will be awarded to a sponsor of a chapter with less than five years of experience as a sponsor and who has never attended an AATSP conference. The awardee receives a plaque and a trip to the AATSP annual conference. Reimbursed expenses include travel, lodging during the conference, and meals. A sponsor can only receive this award once.

  • John Temple State Director of the Year Award - The John Temple State Director of the Year Award recipient is presented with a plaque and a trip to the AATSP annual conference. Reimbursed expenses include travel, lodging during the conference, and meals. A State Director can only receive this award once. The winner is selected by a committee based on the State Directors' year-end reports.

Chapter Student Awards - Deadline: May 1, 2017.

  • Premio de Honor - Sponsors of active chapters may award the Premio de Honor to the top graduating senior of the chapter.

  • Certificados de Excelencia - Sponsors of active chapters may award the Certificado de Excelencia to initiated members, winners in the National Spanish Examinations, or to the initiated member who has achieved the highest grade point average in Spanish or Portuguese for that school year. Duplicate certificates are awarded in case of ties. The number awarded is limited to 10% of current chapter initiates.

  • Certificados por Servicios - Sponsors of active chapters may award the Certificado por Servicios to recognize initiated members who have rendered meritorious service to their Chapter. The number awarded is limited to 10% of current chapter initiates.

    •
These certficates are to honor your members, but they do not take the place of membership dues which must be purchased yearly for each new initiate. Certificates are available at no charge.

SHH-Sigma Delta Pi Awards - Deadline: June 1, 2017.

Albricias Call for Submissions

Albricias, the student journal of the SHH is now accepting submissions for the November 2017 issue. All SHH members are encouraged to submit works of poetry, prose, or original art (including photos) by June 1, 2017.
Theme: Celebraciones
For each issue, a selection committee will choose the most outstanding contributions to Albricias in the categories of poetry, prose, and art/photography. Winners in each category will receive a check in the following amounts in recognition of their contribution to the journal: first place ($250), second place ($100), and third place ($50).
