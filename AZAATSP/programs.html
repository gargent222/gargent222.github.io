Programs For Teachers and Students

The AATSP and the Arizona Chapter offer several programs that benefit both the member teacher and their students. The Sociedad Honoraria Hispanica (SHH) and The National Spanish Exam (NSE) are two programs that Arizona member teachers can provide for their students.

Sociedad Honoraria Hispanica

All secondary schools of recognized standing are eligible to charter a chapter of this honors society which promotes high achievement as well as cultural exploration. Any student who has maintained an honor average in the study of Spanish or Portuguese in the high school for a minimum of three semesters is eligible. The SHH awards numerous scholarships and prizes to student members and chapter sponsors.

Teachers who want to charter and sponsor an SHH chapter should submit a completed application form to the AATSP National Office along with a one-time chartering fee of $35.00. A one-time fee of $5.00 per student must be paid before students can be inducted. The student membership application form is available online. Membership fees can be paid online or by a check sent to the AATSP National Office. All awards, merchandise, recognition items, induction scripts, and chapter bylaws can be found on the AATSP website.

¡Albricias!

The SHH publishes the journal ¡Albricias! which contains essays, poetry, and short articles as well as photography and artwork created by student members of the SHH. ¡Albricias! is distributed to chapter sponsors four times per year and is permanently archived on the AATSP website. Cash awards are presented to students for contributions that are published.

National Spanish Exam

The National Spanish Examinations are online standardized assessment tools to measure student proficiency and achievement in Spanish. The NSE measures achievement by content standards such as vocabulary and grammar as well as proficiency in reading and listening. Registrations begin annually in November and the test is administered in Spring. AATSP member teachers do not have to pay the $75 cost. There is a small per student fee.

There are several student prizes and recognition levels. Certificates for national recognition are available on the AATSP website. In addition, students scoring at or above the 75th percentile are eligible to appy for Senior Scholarships, Junior Travel Awards, and Global Citizen Scholarships (Grades 8, 9, 10).
